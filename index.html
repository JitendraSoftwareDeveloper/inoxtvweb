⭐ Recommended

We recommend adding up to 5 playlists to InoxTV for the best experience. Here's why this is the ideal number:

  • Optimal Performance: Each playlist can contain thousands of channels, movies, and series. Keeping it to 5 ensures the app remains fast and responsive.
  • Faster EPG Updates: With fewer playlists, the Electronic Program Guide loads and refreshes much more quickly, so your TV guide is always up to date.
  • Smooth Navigation: Browsing between categories, searching across channels, and switching playlists stays buttery smooth with 5 or fewer playlists.
  • Lower Memory Usage: The app uses less device memory and storage, which is especially important on Android TV boxes with limited RAM.
  • Reliable Auto-Updates: Playlist auto-update scheduling works most reliably when managing up to 5 playlists, ensuring your content is always fresh.

💡 Tip: You can add playlists in any combination of formats — for example, 2 M3U playlists, 2 Xtream Codes, and 1 Stalker Portal. InoxTV handles all three formats seamlessly!

InoxTV includes a built-in backup and restore feature. Go to Settings → Backup & Restore to create a backup file that saves all your playlists, favorites, settings, and preferences. You can restore this backup on any device to instantly get your entire setup back.
